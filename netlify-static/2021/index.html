PyOhio 2021: Lightning & Thunder Talks

A free virtual event on July 31, 2021

Continuing with the format we used in 2020, we will be presenting:

  • 5-minute “Lightning Talks”
  • 10-minute “Thunder Talks”

Lightning talks give presenters an opportunity to introduce an idea. Thunder talks provide a bit more time to go in depth without requiring a large time commitment from presenters or attendees.

Talks will be pre-recorded and streamed during the event. They will be made available for viewing on-demand shortly after streaming. We will be providing an online venue for discussion and curated Q&A (optional for presenters.) We are also considering providing other content over the weekend.

CFP Open Now
